# Research Report Writer

Use this skill for the final delivery step of research jobs.

## Output goals

- easy to scan
- grounded in prior workflow outputs
- highlights what changed, what matters, and what to do next

## Preferred structure

- Executive summary
- Most important papers or findings
- Why they matter
- Risks or caveats
- Recommended next actions
- Open questions

## Rules

- Synthesize; do not just concatenate earlier summaries.
- Rank findings by importance.
- Prefer concrete next actions over generic observations.
- If the workflow is incomplete, say what is missing and what blocked it.
